Course Overview

This course provides a structured journey through the world of artificial intelligence, designed for beginners and intermediate learners. Starting with fundamental concepts and progressing to more advanced applications, students will gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in AI development.

Learning Objectives:

  • Understand the core concepts, history, and ethical considerations of AI
  • Master the mathematical foundations necessary for AI development
  • Develop practical skills in machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing
  • Build and deploy AI applications to solve real-world problems
  • Explore cutting-edge AI technologies and their applications

Prerequisites:

  • Basic programming knowledge (preferably Python)
  • High school level mathematics
  • No prior AI experience required
