Real-World Applications

Understanding how Actimize components work in practice helps developers build more effective integrations and customizations. Here are common scenarios where the platform provides significant value.

Scenario 1: Trade-Based Money Laundering

Challenge: Criminal organization using over/under-invoicing in international trade to move illicit funds.

Actimize Solution:
  • RCM: Monitors trade finance transactions for pricing anomalies
  • CDD: Flags customers with unusual trade patterns vs. business profile
  • ERCM: Correlates multiple suspicious trades into comprehensive case
  • SAM: Files detailed SAR with trade-specific regulatory requirements
Outcome: 73% reduction in investigation time, 45% increase in detected trade-based ML schemes

Scenario 2: Sanctions Compliance

Challenge: Ensuring comprehensive sanctions screening across all customer touchpoints and transaction types.

Actimize Solution:
  • WLF: Real-time screening with advanced fuzzy matching
  • CDD: Ongoing monitoring of customer sanctions status
  • RCM: Transaction pattern analysis for sanctions evasion
  • ERCM: Rapid investigation of potential sanctions violations
Outcome: 99.7% screening accuracy, 60% reduction in false positive investigations

Scenario 3: Cryptocurrency Integration

Challenge: Monitoring crypto-to-fiat transactions for AML compliance as digital assets become mainstream.

Actimize Solution:
  • RCM: Custom rules for crypto transaction patterns
  • CDD: Enhanced due diligence for crypto-active customers
  • WLF: Screening against crypto-specific sanctions lists
  • ERCM: Specialized investigation tools for blockchain analysis
Outcome: Seamless integration of crypto monitoring with traditional AML processes
